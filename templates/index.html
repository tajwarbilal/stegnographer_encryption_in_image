What is steganography?

Steganography is the act of hiding an encrypted secret data in an image for example which would need a decryption to get the secret data out. Steganography can hide many data including text, image, video, or audio content; the data to be hidden can be hidden inside almost any other type of digital content (Semilof & Clark, 2021).
